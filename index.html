From scattered ideas to a publish-ready authority asset.

Six production stages. Each one ends with something real you can hold, review, and approve — not a status update.

Positioning memo, thesis, reader definition, working title, and full chapter architecture. A manuscript is not enough — the book needs positioning, and it is set here, in writing, before a single chapter is drafted.
